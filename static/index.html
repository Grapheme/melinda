What is it?

Melinda — is a Chrome-compatible experimental web application created by the members of Grapheme team. It lets you create Scopes — interactive kaleidoscopic visualizations that are moving along with the music.

How it works?

Just drag-n-drop your favourite image to change the pattern of a kaleidoscope and do same trick to change the music as well. You can now share the link to your scope and tell everyone how awesome you are!
